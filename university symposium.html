About the Symposium

TechNova 2024 is a national-level technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science. Our mission is to provide a platform for students, researchers, and professionals to share knowledge, explore emerging technologies, and foster a spirit of innovation.

Knowledge Exchange

Present research papers, attend expert talks, and learn from the best in the industry.

Networking

Connect with peers, industry leaders, and potential collaborators from across the country.

Innovation

Participate in challenging competitions like hackathons and project expos to showcase your skills.

Symposium Tracks & Events

A diverse range of events tailored to ignite your passion for technology.

Paper Presentation

Showcase your research and academic findings to a panel of esteemed judges and peers.

Hackathon: CodeSprint

Form a team and build innovative solutions to real-world problems in a high-energy environment.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on cutting-edge topics like AI/ML, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Computing.

Poster Session

Visually present your projects and research posters to a broad audience.

Robotics Challenge

A thrilling competition to test your robotics design and programming skills.

Gaming Tournament

Compete against the best gamers for prizes and bragging rights.
